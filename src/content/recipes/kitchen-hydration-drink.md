# Kitchen Hydration Drink

_Electrolyte refresher for hot environments_

---

## Ingredients

**Base**

- 240ml water
- 15–20ml citrus juice (see flavouring options)
- 5–10ml simple syrup
- 0.5g salt
- 0.25g baking soda (optional)

**Flavouring Options — Citrus (choose one or combine)**

- Lime juice
- Lemon juice
- Yuzu juice
- Orange juice (reduce syrup slightly)
- Grapefruit juice
- Calamansi juice

**Flavouring Options — Additions**

- 30ml cucumber juice
- 120ml coconut water (replacing half the plain water)
- 5ml fresh ginger juice or 2–3 slices muddled
- 4–5 mint leaves, muddled
- 2–3 drops orange blossom or rose water
- 30ml watermelon juice, strained
- 15ml pineapple juice

---

## Method

1. Combine base ingredients and chosen flavourings in a deli container or jar.
2. Add a handful of ice.
3. Seal and shake well until cold.
4. Drink immediately or keep on ice.

---

## Timeline

| Step      | Action              | Duration   |
| --------- | ------------------- | ---------- |
| 1         | Combine ingredients | 1 min      |
| 2–3       | Add ice and shake   | 30 sec     |
| **Total** |                     | **~2 min** |

---

## Notes

- Adjust sweetness to taste — more syrup if using tart citrus, less with sweeter fruits.
- The salt is functional, not for flavour — don't skip it.
- Make a larger batch and keep in a jug on ice for easy refills throughout a shift.
